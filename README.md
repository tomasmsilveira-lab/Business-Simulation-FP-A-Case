# Business-Simulation-FP-A-Case
Business Simulation & FP&A Case
Financial planning & analysis (FP&A) case study focused on comparing growth vs conservative strategies using a business simulation, KPI dashboard, and management‑style recommendations.

Project overview
This project simulates a company’s financial performance under different strategic choices and translates the results into an executive‑ready KPI dashboard.
It is designed to showcase practical FP&A skills: scenario modelling, performance measurement, and clear communication of data‑driven recommendations for management.

Key features
Business simulation model with at least two strategy paths (growth vs conservative) affecting revenue, costs, and profitability over multiple periods.
KPI dashboard summarising key metrics such as revenue growth, margin, cash flow, and other business‑specific indicators by scenario.
Management‑style summary report highlighting insights, trade‑offs, and recommended strategic actions based on the data.
Clean layout aimed at non‑technical stakeholders, focusing on clarity and decision support rather than technical detail.

Repository structure
Business_Simulation_FP&A.xlsx – Main Excel file containing:
     Assumptions & drivers for each strategy scenario.
     Simulated financial statements or key line items by period and scenario.
     KPI dashboard (charts and tables) designed for management review.
     Short management‑style report sheet with findings and recommendations.

Methodology
Scenario design: Define at least two strategic cases (growth vs conservative) with different assumptions for pricing, volume, operating costs, and investments.
Simulation: Project period‑by‑period results (e.g., revenue, EBIT, cash flow) for each scenario using consistent formulas and drivers.
KPI selection: Focus on a core set of metrics relevant to management: growth, profitability, cash generation, and any operational KPIs used in the case.
Reporting: Translate the numeric results into a concise management‑style narrative that explains the impact of each strategy and recommends a course of action.

How to use
Download and open Business_Simulation_FP&A_Case.xlsx.
Start in the Scenarios sheet to review or adjust the drivers for the growth and conservative strategies.
In the Assumptions sheet choose the strategy.
Navigate to the Dashboard sheet to see how the different strategies impact the main KPIs over time.
Read the Final_Report sheet for a short, presentation‑ready explanation of the results and recommendations.

Skills demonstrated
Financial planning & analysis (FP&A) and scenario modelling.
KPI design and dashboard building for business stakeholders.
Turning quantitative results into a clear, management‑style narrative with data‑driven recommendations.
